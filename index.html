About Telangana Blood Network

Telangana Blood Network is a non-profit initiative dedicated to connecting blood donors with patients in need across Telangana. Our mission is to ensure that no patient dies due to unavailability of blood.

Founded in 2023, we have facilitated over 10,000 blood donations across all districts of Telangana. Our network includes voluntary donors, hospitals, and blood banks working together to save lives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Generally, donors must be between 18-65 years old, weigh at least 50kg, and be in good health. There should be at least 56 days between donations for males and 84 days for females.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, blood donation is completely safe. Sterile, disposable equipment is used for each donor, so there's no risk of contracting any infection.

How long does the donation process take?

The entire process takes about 45 minutes to an hour, with the actual blood donation taking only 8-10 minutes.

What should I do before donating blood?

Eat a healthy meal before donating, drink plenty of fluids, and get a good night's sleep. Avoid fatty foods before donation as they can affect blood tests.
